Plain Dosa

Plain Dosa is a classic South Indian dish that is a thin, crispy pancake made from a fermented batter of rice and black gram. It is served hot, often with chutney and sambar.
The dish is known for its simplicity and versatility, and it can be enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.
Price: $9.49

Masala Dosa

Masala dosa is a popular South Indian dish, typically made from a fermented batter of rice and legumes, such as black gram, along with various spices like fenugreek and red chili.
It is often served with a spiced potato filling, chutney, sambar, and potato curry.
Price: $10.49

Mysore Masala Dosa

Mysore masala dosa is a popular South Indian dish made from a fermented batter of rice and lentils. It is characterized by its crispy exterior and soft, fluffy interior.
The dosa is typically filled with a spicy potato mixture and served with sambar and coconut chutney.
Price: $11.49


Idli & Sambar

Idli and sambar are a classic South Indian breakfast combination, where fluffy soft idli (savory steamed rice cakes) are served with a flavorful lentil and vegetable stew called sambar.
This dish is popular for its comforting taste and nutritional benefits, making it a staple in many households.
Price: $9.50

Paddu & Chutney

Paddu is a traditional South Indian dish, often served as a breakfast or snack, and is typically made with a fermented batter consisting of rice, lentils, and sometimes beaten rice.
It is shallow-fried in a special mold called a paddu mold or appe pan and is commonly served with chutneys like tomato-onion chutney or coconut chutney.
Price: $7.49
